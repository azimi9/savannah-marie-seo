

# SAVANNAH MARIE — SEO Expert Personal Brand Website

## Overview
A modern, high-converting personal brand website for Savannah Marie, an SEO Expert & Organic Growth Strategist based in Texas. The site will feature a premium, trustworthy design with white backgrounds, soft green/blue accents, strong typography, and conversion-focused CTAs throughout.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with bold headline ("Rank Higher. Get More Traffic. Grow Faster."), value proposition, and two CTA buttons (Book a Free SEO Audit / Get Strategy Call)
- **Services overview** — grid of 8 core services with icons and short descriptions
- **Results/stats bar** — key metrics (e.g., "500+ Keywords Ranked", "200% Avg Traffic Growth")
- **Testimonials carousel** — client quotes with names, roles, and star ratings
- **Case study highlights** — 2-3 mini cards showing before/after results
- **Contact CTA section** — final conversion block with form or call-to-action

### 2. About Page
- Professional bio and photo placeholder for Savannah Marie
- Experience breakdown: Technical SEO, On-Page, Off-Page, Local SEO
- Mission & vision statements
- "Why Choose Savannah" section with key differentiators
- Tools & certifications grid (Google Analytics, Search Console, Ahrefs, SEMrush, etc.)

### 3. Services Page
- 8 detailed service sections: Technical SEO, On-Page SEO, Local SEO, SEO Audit & Strategy, Keyword Research, Link Building, Content Optimization, Monthly SEO Management
- Each service includes: description, benefits list, process steps, and a CTA button
- Clean card-based layout with icons

### 4. Case Studies Page
- Case study cards with before/after metrics (traffic, rankings, ROI)
- Placeholder areas for screenshots/charts
- Results summary with percentage improvements
- CTA to get similar results

### 5. Blog Page
- Blog listing with category filters (SEO Tips, Keyword Research, Algorithm Updates, Small Business SEO)
- Card layout with title, excerpt, date, and category tag
- Sample blog post entries with placeholder content

### 6. Contact Page
- Contact form: Name, Email, Business Name, Website URL, Message
- Client-side validation with proper error messages
- Business contact info displayed: email, phone, address
- "Book Your Free SEO Strategy Call" CTA
- Embedded map placeholder for Texas location

### 7. Legal Pages (4 pages)
- **Privacy Policy** — data collection, GDPR compliance, Google Analytics usage, contact info
- **Terms & Conditions** — intellectual property, limitation of liability, governing law
- **Disclaimer** — general disclaimer, earnings disclaimer, professional advice disclaimer
- **Cookie Policy** — cookie types, how to manage cookies, third-party cookies

---

## Global Design & Layout
- **Sticky navigation** with logo, page links, and a highlighted CTA button
- **Footer** with quick links, service links, legal page links, contact info, and social placeholders
- **Color scheme**: White background, dark text, soft green/teal accent colors
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with strong heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- **Mobile responsive** design throughout all pages
- **Smooth scroll** and subtle animations for engagement

## Technical Notes
- All pages are static/frontend-only (no backend needed)
- Contact form will show a success toast (no email sending without backend integration)
- SEO-friendly heading structure and semantic HTML
- Internal linking between pages
